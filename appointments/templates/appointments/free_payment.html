About the FREE Subscription

$0/month   |   No Savings

  • Up to 20 Clients/Month
  • Up to 50 Appointments/Month
  • Email Support
  • Basic Analytics

Our Free Subscription plan is perfect for doctors looking to get started with our platform at no initial cost. It allows you to manage 50 appointments per month, connect with a single doctor profile, and access basic analytical tools to track your practice.

This is an excellent option if you’re just testing the waters and want a risk-free way to explore our core features. You’ll have email support available for quick assistance, along with simple analytics to help you understand your patient flow.

For enhanced capabilities—like managing more appointments or linking multiple doctors—explore our paid subscriptions. But if you’re looking for a simple, zero-cost solution to keep your practice organized, this Free plan is a reliable choice.
